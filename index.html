How to Use This List
Use the search box to find a specific facility or employer. The NB Requirements column tells you exactly what submission method applies. INF = currently In Force policies at that facility. OOF = Out of Force (lapsed). High INF counts indicate active, high-density worksites — prioritise these.
⚠️ Submission Requirements
Qlink / BilliE — No physical lodgement required. Submit via Qlink as normal.
STO Mandate & Payslip — Physical stop order mandate and payslip required. Lodgement must be confirmed with a counter-signed STO mandate or stamp and signed STO letter from the facility.
DO Collection (Worksite Restricted) — Debit order collection only at these facilities. Stop order is not available.
